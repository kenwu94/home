Budget Mouse

In my opinion, the best mouse for people on a tight budget is the HyperX Pulsefire FPS for a price of $40. This mouse is rated for 50 million clicks which will last 10 years on average which will give you enough time to get a better mouse if desired assuming you don't break it before then. This mouse has led lighting which makes it a bit better looking. It has an ergonomic form wihch makes it a little bit more comfortabsle to use. It also includes a DPI switch that goes up to 3200 DPI but normally you will only be using 400 or 800 DPI.
